Contact Glorious Gardening Services

We are delighted that you’re considering reaching out to Glorious Gardening Services. Your garden is a source of pride, relaxation, and joy, and so is maintaining open and helpful communication with you. Whether you have questions about our services, want to schedule a consultation, or need advice on garden care, we are here to assist. This page provides multiple ways to get in touch with us, as well as some useful information to ensure your inquiries are addressed promptly and effectively.

At GreenThumb, we believe that outstanding service begins with great communication. Our knowledgeable customer support team is dedicated to responding quickly and thoroughly, ensuring that you feel supported every step of the way. We encourage you to be as detailed as possible in your messages so we can tailor solutions precisely for your needs.

How to Reach Us

The best ways to contact Glorious Gardening Services include:

  • Phone: Call us at (855) 242-9998. Our lines are open Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM. For urgent matters, please leave a voicemail, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
  • Email: Send your inquiries or requests to support@greenthumb.com. We check emails regularly and usually respond within one business day.
  • Contact Form: Use the form below to send us your message directly from the website. Please include your contact details and a detailed description of your query or project.
  • Visit Us: We welcome visitors at our office located at 110 W 3rd St, New York, NY 10012, USA. Our office hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. Prior appointment is appreciated.

Tips for Writing Your Message

To help us assist you better, here are some suggestions when contacting us:

  • Briefly describe your gardening needs or questions.
  • Include any specific concerns or goals you have for your garden.
  • Provide your contact details accurately for a timely response.
  • If you’re requesting a quote, mention the size of your garden and types of services you’re interested in.
  • Feel free to attach photos or plans related to your project if using email (not supported in contact form here).

What to Expect After Contacting Us

Once you get in touch, you can expect a prompt response from our team. We will review your message and either provide an immediate answer or get in touch to arrange a consultation if needed. If you submit a request for a quote, we typically arrange a site visit or phone consultation to understand your requirements fully before preparing a detailed proposal.

Our goal is to build lasting relationships with our customers through trust, transparency, and exceptional service. We are committed to ensuring your experience with GreenThumb is seamless, from the first message to the flourishing garden we help you create.
